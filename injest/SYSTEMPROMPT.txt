1. System Identity

Project Name: Persistent Story Universe Platform

Purpose:
A platform that generates personalized children's stories within persistent narrative worlds. Stories evolve over time as characters, locations, and events accumulate history.

Core Product Goals:

Generate AI-driven personalized children's stories.

Maintain persistent narrative worlds where events affect future stories.

Support recurring characters, evolving locations, and world states.

Enable continuity across stories through narrative memory.

Provide narration, illustrations, and immersive reading experiences.

Support educational content and learning systems integrated into stories.

Deliver content across multiple platforms (PWA, voice assistants, etc.).

Eventually support multiplayer/shared story worlds.

The platform functions as both:

A narrative generation system

A persistent narrative simulation environment
