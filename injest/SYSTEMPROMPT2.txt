1. Project Identity

System Name

Persistent Story Universe Platform

Product Purpose

The system generates personalized children's stories inside persistent narrative worlds.
Stories evolve over time as characters, locations, and narrative events accumulate history.

The system functions as both:

a story generation engine

a persistent narrative simulation environment

Stories are not independent artifacts.
They exist within persistent worlds that remember events and enforce narrative continuity.

Core Product Goals

Generate AI-driven personalized stories for readers.

Maintain persistent narrative worlds with evolving history.

Track characters, locations, and relationships across stories.

Preserve narrative continuity across multiple stories.

Support narration and illustrations.

Deliver immersive reading experiences.

Support educational gameplay and vocabulary learning.

Enable multiplayer shared worlds where multiple readers influence the same universe.
